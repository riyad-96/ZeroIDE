Meet ZeroIDE — Your Coding Companion

Hey, I’m ZeroIDE — a lightweight, no-install web-based code editor built to help you code faster, cleaner, and with style. Think of me as that chill coding buddy who never crashes, always remembers your work, and never judges your typos.

Whether you're experimenting with HTML/CSS/JS snippets, testing ideas in seconds, or just learning the ropes — I’m here to make that process buttery smooth. From live previews to CDN tag insertion, custom fonts & themes, and keyboard shortcuts — I’ve got the right tools in the right places.

I live inside your browser, and everything you do stays with you. No signups, no uploads, no distractions — just you and your ideas.

I was handcrafted by a passionate developer (yep, that’s Riyad), and I’m always evolving. Let’s build cool stuff together.

Built with love, logic & zero distractions — by a developer who just wanted a better way to code.
